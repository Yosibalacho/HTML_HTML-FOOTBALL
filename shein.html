Bershka (Spanish: [beɾʃka, beɾska]) is a clothing retailer company created in 1998 in Spain. It is part of the Spanish Inditex group (which also owns the brands of Zara, Massimo Dutti, Pull&Bear, Oysho, Uterqüe, Stradivarius and Zara Home).
